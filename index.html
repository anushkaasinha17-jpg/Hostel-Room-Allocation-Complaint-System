What We Do

Two Systems. One Platform.

01
🏠

Room Allocation

Automated assignment of hostel rooms based on student preferences, year, branch, and availability. Real-time room status tracking with admin override controls.

  • ➔ Preference-based allocation
  • ➔ Waitlist management
  • ➔ Roommate pairing
  • ➔ Semester-wise renewal
02
🔧

Complaint Tracking

Students raise maintenance requests directly from their dashboard. Wardens and staff receive real-time alerts and can update resolution status instantly.

  • ➔ Categorized complaints
  • ➔ Priority escalation
  • ➔ Photo uploads
  • ➔ Resolution timeline
📊

Live Dashboard

Admin & warden dashboards with real-time occupancy, complaint metrics, and room status.

🔔

Notifications

Email & SMS alerts for room assignments, complaint updates, and warden announcements.

🔐

Role-Based Access

Separate portals for Students, Wardens, Maintenance Staff, and Administrators.
